the soviet union, officially the union of soviet socialist republics, was a transcontinental country that spanned much of eurasia from 1922 to 1991. the soviet union produced many significant social and technological achievements and innovations. since the start of the great patriotic war at the latest, abbreviating the russian name of the soviet union as has been complete taboo, the reason being that as a russian cyrillic abbreviation is instead associated with the infamous of nazi germany, just as ss is in english.
