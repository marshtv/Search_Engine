several facts about the olympics. the early games were a religious festival from 776 b.c. to 393 a.d. in 393 a.d. they became outlawed for being a pagan festival. baron pierre de coubertin started a revival of the games in 1894 a.k.a the modern olympics. in 1924 in chamonix, france, the first winter games were held. 1912 saw the last olympic gold medals made entirely out of gold. spyros samaras composed the olympic hymn and kostis palamas added the words. in the 1896 athens olympic games, the hymn was first used, but the ioc only declared it the official anthem in 1957.
